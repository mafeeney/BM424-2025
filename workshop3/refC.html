32  Group C - References

Tip

The following list of references is provided as a starting place for you to begin exploring various aspects of Staphylococcus aureus biology, diagnostics, epidemiology, and treatment. However, this is by no means an exhaustive list: you should further explore the relevant peer-reviewed literature independently.

  • Selected Staphylococcus aureus Diagnostics and Treatment Literature
  • Selected Staphylococcus aureus Epidemiology Literature

  • Staphylococcus aureus bacteraemia (NHS)

  • Prevention and control of infection in care homes – an information resource (Health Protection Agency)

  • Health protection in children and young people settings, including education

  • Infection control in the built environment

  • MRSA guidelines (Healthcare Infection Society)

  • CDC MRSA Factsheets and Posters for different audiences

  • Communication campaigns on antimicrobial resistance (European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control)
